Website social Tabs

Odoo website social Tabs module is deployed for the ease of the eCommerce holders who can configure their profiles on social networking sites with their eCommerce website directly. Shared posts, offers and promotional schemes on social media can be accessed through your website at one place on a single click. Let your customers follow you on various social networking sites for your recent updates. Giving access to your social media profiles will increase your sales count and followers on the social media. By default, using this module you can share your profile links to twitter, youtube, facebook, Vimeo, Flicker, Google+, Pinterest, flike, you may also customize one page for sharing your own piece of information.

Salient Features
  • It is a very user friendly module to configure your account on various social networking sites.
  • This module is the one step solution to most of the social networking sites.
  • Admin can configure social networking sites through simple steps.
  • On a single click, without directing to other webpage customer can follow you on social networking sites.
  • Posts shared on social media can be accessed on your eCommerce website in a small window.
  • You can create your own custom tabs by simply drag and drop.
  • Admin can set the position of the tabs to the right or left of the web page.
  • Admin has the option how to display the social tabs on website.

Create a Tab

Some key points

  • Create a social tab or a custom tab.
  • Simply click on add an item and create the tab.
  • Select the media type (social tab or custom tab) you want to create.
  • Choose an icon for the tab.
General Information

Some key points

  • For creation of a social tab fill the required information
  • Select a social tab from the selection box.the selection box contains nine social tabs like facebook(posts) , facebook like, youtube, viemo, tumblr, flikr, google+, stumbleupon and pinterest.
  • You can customize the tab by simply adding the color of the tab window in website ,limit of the posts to display , Title of the tab etc.
